Privacy Policy

Last updated: November 19, 2025

1. Introduction

Welcome to My Dream Consultancy Ltd. We are committed to protecting your privacy and personal data. This Privacy Policy outlines how My Dream Consultancy Ltd collects, uses, maintains, and discloses information collected from users of our website and services.

2. Personal Identification Information

We may collect personal identification information in a variety of ways, including when users visit our site, register, place an order, subscribe to a newsletter, respond to a survey, or fill out a form. Users may be asked for name, email address, mailing address, phone number, or payment info. We collect this only when users voluntarily submit it.

3. Non-Personal Identification Information

We may collect non-personal information such as browser type, device, operating system, and ISP whenever users interact with the site.

4. Web Browser Cookies

We may use cookies to enhance user experience. Users can refuse cookies via their browser settings, but some parts of the site may not function properly.

5. How We Use Collected Information

  • Improve customer service and respond to support needs.
  • Personalize user experience and understand how users as a group use the site.
  • Improve our site based on your feedback.
  • Process transactions and provide services requested.
  • Administer contests, promotions, surveys, or features.
  • Send periodic emails about orders, updates, or company news (with opt-out available).

6. How We Protect Your Information

We use appropriate data collection, storage, and processing practices and security measures to protect against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction of personal information. Sensitive data is transmitted over SSL where applicable.

7. Sharing Your Personal Information

We do not sell, trade, or rent personal identification information. We may share aggregated demographic info with partners. We may use third-party providers to help operate the site or administer activities (e.g., newsletters), and may share info for those limited purposes with your permission.

8. Third-Party Websites

Our site may link to other sites. Their content and practices are not under our control and are subject to their own policies.

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this policy at any time. The updated date will appear at the top of this page. Please review periodically to stay informed.

10. Your Acceptance

By using this site, you accept this policy and our terms. If you do not agree, please do not use the site. Continued use after changes constitutes acceptance.

11. Contacting Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, contact us at info@mydreamconsultancy.com.
