Flights from London to Paris: Quick City Break Guide

Paris remains the ultimate weekend getaway, with frequent flights from every major London airport. Morning departures are perfect for travelers who want a full first day in the city.

Route Snapshot

  • Flight time: Roughly 1 hour 15 minutes direct.
  • Top tip: Aim for mid-week travel to secure the lowest fares and lighter airport queues.
  • Arrival: Charles de Gaulle (CDG) offers direct rail links into central Paris.

City Break Planning

Book museum tickets in advance and consider staying in the 1st–7th arrondissements for quick access to the Seine and iconic landmarks.
