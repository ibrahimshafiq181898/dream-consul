Flights from London to Dubai: Premium & Value Options

Dubai blends futuristic skylines with desert adventures, making it a year-round favorite. Expect multiple daily departures from Heathrow, Gatwick, and Stansted with a mix of full-service and low-cost carriers.

Route Highlights

  • Flight time: Around 7 hours direct, with overnight departures ideal for maximizing arrival-day time.
  • Best booking window: 10–12 weeks out for the best balance of seat choice and price.
  • Airport transfers: Dubai Metro connects DXB to Downtown and Marina; taxis are readily available 24/7.

When to Travel

For mild weather, aim for November through March. Shoulder months (April and October) often deliver attractive fares and fewer crowds.
