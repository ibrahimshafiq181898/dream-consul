Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about flights and hotels.

When is the best time to book a flight?

Booking 2–3 months in advance for domestic and 4–6 months for international routes typically yields the best prices.

What should I look for when choosing a hotel?

Consider location, recent reviews, included amenities, and cancellation flexibility.

How far in advance should I book hotels?

For peak seasons, book 2–4 months ahead. Otherwise, 2–6 weeks often balances price and availability.

What are the best days to fly for cheaper prices?

Tuesdays and Wednesdays often have lower fares compared to weekends. Always compare across several days.

How can I find the best hotel deals?

Use price alerts, compare multiple providers, and check direct offers or members-only rates.

What documents do I need for international travel?

Valid passport, visa if required, and any health documents or travel insurance depending on your destination.
